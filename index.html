Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2025

KotoDex respects your privacy. This document explains what data we access, how it is used, and what choices you have.

📲 Data Collection

KotoDex does not collect or store personal information.

🔐 Google Sign-In and Google Sheets Access

KotoDex uses Google Sign-In only to access the user's own Google Sheets.

  • We do not access or collect your name, email, or profile.
  • A temporary authentication token is used to interact with the Google Sheets API.
  • This token is used during your session only and is not shared.
  • No data is transmitted to any external server outside of Google's APIs.

You are always in full control of your data, which remains in your Google account.

🚫 Data Sharing

KotoDex does not share any data with third parties.

❓ Questions

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, feel free to reach out.
